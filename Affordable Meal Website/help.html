🆘 Help & Support

Find quick answers or get in touch with us

How do I place an order?

1. Browse our menu and add meals to cart

2. Go to Cart page

3. Review items and click "Place Order"

4. Complete payment and track your order

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above ₹300.

Below ₹300 → ₹30 flat charge

Priority delivery (faster) → +₹10

How do subscriptions work?

Choose Weekly (₹500) or Monthly (₹1800) plan.

Get 1 meal/day for the selected duration.

Auto-renews, cancel anytime from your profile.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing.

Contact support immediately for changes.

After preparation starts, no cancellations possible.

How do I pay?

Options: UPI, Credit/Debit Card, Cash on Delivery, Wallet

All payments are secure and encrypted.

What if my order is late?

We track every order in real-time.

If delayed >10 min, you'll get ₹20 discount on next order.

Contact us if delay is longer.

Do you have Jain / Veg-only meals?

Yes! All our meals have clear Veg/Jain labels.

We have dedicated Jain kitchen for pure Jain meals.
